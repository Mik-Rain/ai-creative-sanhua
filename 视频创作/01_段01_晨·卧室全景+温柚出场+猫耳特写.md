# 段 01 · 晨·卧室全景 + 温柚出场 + 猫耳特写(9s)

> ⏱ **总时长**:9 秒(3+4+2)· 包含原镜 01 / 02 / 03
> 🎬 **平台设置建议**:10s 上限,选 9s 或 10s(末尾留 1s 静止)
> 📍 **段落位置**:**开场** —— 整个故事的第一个镜头组,需要定调"慢、柔、轻"

## 段落大意

```
[0.0s] 卧室全景 · 纱帘光斑 · 床铺凌乱 · 暖金色清晨光
[3.0s] 慢推入 · 温柚半靠白色枕头堆 · 露肩毛衣 · 琥珀睡眼
[7.0s] 特写 · 猫耳随头轻轻偏动 · 耳尖橘色绒毛在阳光下发亮
[9.0s] 静止 1s · 耳尖绒毛仍带微微颤动
```

## 完整 Prompt(英文 · 建议直贴)

```text
Anime catgirl "Wen You", 18yo, half-human half-cat. Hair: waist-length
tri-color (black, white, orange) loose waves, slightly messy. Eyes:
amber, half-lidded dreamy sleepy. Cat ears: tri-color fluffy (black/
white/orange patches), twitch gently. Tail: long fluffy tri-color,
hidden under duvet now. Outfit: oversized off-shoulder cream-apricot
chunky-knit sweater, one shoulder slipped down. Skin: fair with warm
undertones, soft. Style: semi-realistic anime, painterly, soft warm
light, warm color palette (cream, gold, peach).

A continuous 9-second shot at dawn inside a serene anime-style
bedroom. [0-3s] Wide establishing shot: sheer white curtains half
drawn, warm golden morning sunlight streaming through, casting long
stripe-shaped light patches on a plush unmade bed with rumpled white
duvet, a subtle human silhouette visible under the covers, a small
white ceramic vase on a wooden nightstand. Slow forward dolly from
the door perspective, dust particles float slowly. [3-7s] Medium
shot: the catgirl Wen You reclining lazily in a pile of fluffy white
pillows, sweater slipping off one shoulder, tri-color hair spread
over shoulders and pillow, one hand lightly touching her cheek,
amber eyes half-lidded, dreamy and sleepy. Slow push-in toward her
upper body, soft side-back rim light. [7-9s] Close-up of her head
and ears: she tilts her head slightly, tri-color cat ears gently
twitch and rotate, ear-tip orange fur glowing translucent gold in
direct sunlight, hair strands brush past the ear edges. Fixed
camera with subtle breathing micro-shake. Painterly anime, soft
bokeh, cinematic, 4K, 24fps, warm tones, low contrast.
```

## 完整 Prompt(中文 · 备选/可灵中文模式)

```text
二次元写实厚涂风,治愈系。
角色温柚:18 岁三花猫耳少女,黑/白/橘三色渐变长发,琥珀色眼眸,
米杏色露肩粗针织毛衣,蓬松三花尾巴(此时藏在被下)。

连续 9 秒单镜头。0-3 秒:卧室清晨全景,米白纱帘半掩,暖金色阳光
投下长条光斑,床铺凌乱,被窝有隆起人影,床头柜白色陶瓷花瓶,空
气浮尘缓慢飘动,从门口缓慢前推。3-7 秒:温柚半靠在白色枕头堆
里,毛衣滑下一侧肩头,三色长发散在肩头与枕面,一手轻抵脸颊,琥
珀色眼眸半眯朦胧带睡意,慢推至人物上半身,侧方晨光形成柔光轮
廓。7-9 秒:猫耳特写,温柚轻偏头,三花猫耳随头轻晃,耳尖橘色
绒毛在直射阳光下根根分明泛金边,固定机位带轻微呼吸感。
```

## 镜头连贯性提示

- **本段开头** = **全片开场**,需奠定整体暖金色调
- **本段末尾**:猫耳微微颤动(可作为段 02 的"起手"线索)
- **接段 02 衔接点**:本段猫耳特写 → 段 02 转为面部睡眼特写
- **剪映转场建议**:本段 9s 末尾 0.5s 自然渐暗 → 段 02 开头 0.3s 从同角度亮起(可省溶解)
