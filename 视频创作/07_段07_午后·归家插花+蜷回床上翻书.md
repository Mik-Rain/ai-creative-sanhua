# 段 07 · 午后·归家插花 + 蜷回床上翻书(6s)

> ⏱ **总时长**:6 秒(原镜 15 占满 4s,补 2s 翻书动作)· 包含原镜 15
> 🎬 **平台设置建议**:6s 或 10s
> 📍 **段落位置**:**第 7 段** —— 午后段,光线从正午过渡到午后暖黄

## 段落大意

```
[0.0s] 全景 · 午后卧室 · 阳光角度偏移变柔
[1.5s] 温柚把洋甘菊插进白色陶瓷花瓶 · 摆床头柜
[3.0s] 温柚蜷回床上 · 抱抱枕翻看画册
[4.5s] 尾巴圈住双腿 · 猫耳懒懒耷拉
[5.5s] 侧头望向窗边花瓶里的洋甘菊
[6.0s] 静止 0.5s
```

## 完整 Prompt(英文 · 建议直贴)

```text
Anime catgirl "Wen You", 18yo, half-human half-cat. Hair: waist-length
tri-color (black, white, orange) loose waves, slightly tousled from
the walk. Eyes: amber, peaceful and content. Cat ears: tri-color
fluffy, drooping lazily, half-folded. Tail: long fluffy tri-color,
wraps around her own legs. Outfit: cream-apricot chunky-knit sweater
plus cream knit long skirt.

A continuous 6-second shot. Wide shot of an afternoon bedroom,
sunlight angle has shifted lower and softer than morning, warm gold
diffused light. Wen You carefully trims a small bunch of chamomile
flowers and places them into a small white ceramic vase on the
windowsill nightstand. She then climbs back onto the bed, curls up
hugging a soft pillow, flips through a picture book, her tri-color
tail wraps around her own legs lazily. She occasionally glances
toward the chamomile flowers in the vase by the window. The
chamomile flowers in the white vase must be clearly visible in the
frame. Slow dolly from doorway to bedside, 0.5s stillness at end.
Painterly anime, warm gold afternoon tones, 4K, 24fps.
```

## 完整 Prompt(中文 · 备选)

```text
二次元写实厚涂风,治愈系。温柚:三花猫耳少女,黑/白/橘三色长发,
琥珀色眼眸,神态平和,米杏色毛衣 + 米色长裙,猫耳懒耷,蓬松尾
巴圈腿。

连续 6 秒。午后卧室全景,阳光角度已偏移变柔,午后暖金漫射光。
温柚把洋甘菊花束修剪插进白色陶瓷花瓶,摆在靠窗床头柜上(花
瓶与花束必须在画面中清晰可见),然后蜷回床上抱抱枕翻看画册,
三花尾巴圈住双腿,偶尔侧头望向窗边盛放的小花。从门口慢推至床
边,末尾 0.5s 静止。
```

## ⚠️ 注意事项

- **光线必须从正午暖白过渡到午后暖黄** —— prompt 中明确写 "afternoon""warm gold""lower angle"
- 白色陶瓷花瓶是**新道具**,首次出现需要明确 "small white ceramic vase" 让 AI 渲染出来
- 猫耳"懒耷"是午后慵懒调性的关键 —— prompt 中明确 "drooping lazily, half-folded"
- 翻画册是**段 7 末尾的过渡动作**,为段 8 蜜桃 + 闲聊铺垫
