## 完整 Prompt(英文 · 建议直贴)

```text
@温柚: Anime catgirl "Wen You", 18yo, half-human half-cat.
Hair: waist-length tri-color (black, white, orange) loose waves.
Eyes: amber, content and squinted with pleasure, then gazing
out the window with soft nostalgia. Cat ears: tri-color fluffy,
relaxed and half-flat during the whole segment. Tail: long
fluffy tri-color, curls around her own legs when reading, hangs
naturally by her side at the window. Outfit: cream-apricot
chunky-knit sweater + cream knit long skirt.

@舒芙蕾: [START FRAME — exact reference for the opening] Close-
up of Wen You squinting in satisfaction, the roommate's palm
under her cheek, soft warm interior dessert-shop light.

@花束特写: A full bunch of fresh chamomile, small white-and-
yellow petals, painterly anime, warm golden light.

A continuous 15-second shot (MAX LIMIT), opening frame MUST match
@舒芙蕾. The lighting transition across this segment is the most
important visual element: afternoon warm yellow (0-7s) → sunset
peach-pink (10-15s). The transition must be SMOOTH, not abrupt.

[0-2s] CLOSE-UP: Wen You nuzzling the roommate's palm, eyes
comfortably squinted, ears half-flat, a small dab of cream still
on her lip. The roommate gently pulls back her hand. Hold the
end of this moment.

[2-5s] SCENE TRANSITION (via cut + dissolve in editing, prompt
focuses on the new scene): AFTERNOON BEDROOM. Sunlight has shifted
to a more slanted angle. Wen You holds the chamomile bunch in
both hands, carefully tucks them into a white ceramic vase on the
wooden nightstand by the window. The vase is the same one from
the opening shot. Slow push-in toward the vase, her tri-color
hair catches the afternoon light.

[5-7s] MEDIUM: Wen You curls back onto the bed, hugging a soft
pillow, flips through a picture book, her tri-color tail curls
around her own legs. Soft side light, overall warm-yellow
diffuse. Lazy and relaxed.

[7-10s] CLOSE-UP: Wen You's fingertips pick up a freshly cut
peach slice, the flesh looks plump and translucent, juice on her
fingertip. She puts it in her mouth, eyes squint comfortably.
Side light catches the peach flesh's wet texture. Fixed close-up.

[10-13s] MEDIUM (lighting now in sunset peach-pink): Wen You sits
by the window, holding the chamomile bouquet again (now freshly
re-cut from the vase? — interpret as she's holding a smaller
sprig of chamomile picked from the original bunch, or interpret
as the bunch itself re-tied loosely), her tri-color tail hangs
naturally by her side. Her face is bathed in the sunset's
warm-pink light, hair strands and ear-tip fur glint with
orange-red-gold edges. She gazes out the window, soft and
nostalgic. Strong backlight from the setting sun, casting long
shadows. Camera slowly orbits from behind her to her side face.

[13-15s] WIDE SHOT PULLING BACK (the closing shot): the camera
slowly pulls away from Wen You, revealing the room. Outside the
window, the sun slowly sinks, sky turning from peach to warm-
brown. The chamomile in the vase on the nightstand glows softly
in the last light. The bed shows faint body indentations. Hold
the last frame as the ENDING keyframe (this is the final frame
of the entire film, use it for the poster).

Painterly anime, lighting transitions from afternoon warm yellow
to sunset peach-pink, soft bokeh, cinematic, 4K, 24fps, 0.5x
motion speed.
```

## 完整 Prompt(中文 · 备选/可灵中文模式)

```text
@温柚:三花猫耳少女温柚,18 岁,黑/白/橘三色长发,琥珀色眼眸(本段内
从满足眯眼→ 到窗边怀旧),蓬松三花猫耳(全程放松半耷)+尾巴(翻书
时圈腿,窗边时垂身侧),米杏色毛衣 + 米色针织长裙。

@舒芙蕾:【**起始帧,必须与本图严格一致**】温柚舒服眯眼近景,好友
掌心托着她脸颊,店内暖光。

@花束特写:一束饱满洋甘菊,金白色小花瓣,厚涂风,暖金光。

连续 15 秒(满上限)。**本段光影渐变是全片最重要的视觉元素**:午后
暖黄(0-7s)→ 夕阳橘粉(10-15s),必须**平滑过渡**。

0-2 秒:近景,温柚蹭好友掌心,眼睛舒服眯起,耳朵半耷,唇角还沾一
点奶油。好友轻收回手。定格这瞬间。

2-5 秒:**场景转换(剪辑时剪映 Dissolve,prompt 聚焦新场景)**。午
后卧室,阳光角度已偏移。温柚双手抱洋甘菊花束,细心修剪后插入床
头柜上的白色陶瓷花瓶(开场就出现的同一只)。慢推至花瓶,三色长发
在午后斜光下泛光泽。

5-7 秒:中景,温柚蜷回床上,抱柔软抱枕,翻看画册,三花尾巴圈住
自己双腿。侧光,午后暖黄漫射,慵懒松弛。

7-10 秒:特写,温柚指尖捻起切好的蜜桃,果肉饱满透亮,汁水沾指尖;
送入口中,眼睛舒服眯起。侧光打在果肉上,水润质感。固定特写。

10-13 秒:中景(光影进入夕阳橘粉阶段)。温柚坐窗边,手捧一束洋甘
菊(可解释为:从花瓶中重新抽出的几支,或原本花束的延续),三花尾巴
垂身侧,侧脸浸在落日柔光里,望向窗外,发丝与耳尖绒毛泛橘红金边。
夕阳侧逆光,长长影子拖出。镜头从她身后缓环绕至侧脸。

13-15 秒:全景缓拉远(终镜)。镜头从温柚身侧拉远,显出整个房间。
窗外夕阳缓缓下沉,天际由橘粉渐变暖棕。床头柜上花瓶里的洋甘菊在
余晖里安静盛放,床上残留浅浅凹陷痕迹。**末帧保持 1 秒静止**(终
镜,可作海报帧)。

整体:厚涂风,光影从午后暖黄平滑过渡到夕阳橘粉,柔焦,电影感,4K,
24fps,0.5x 慢速。
```
