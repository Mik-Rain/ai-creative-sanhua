## 完整 Prompt(英文 · 建议直贴)

```text
@温柚: Anime catgirl "Wen You", 18yo, half-human half-cat.
Hair: waist-length tri-color (black, white, orange) loose waves,
slightly messy. Eyes: amber, half-lidded dreamy sleepy. Cat ears:
tri-color fluffy (black/white/orange patches), twitch gently.
Tail: long fluffy tri-color, hidden under duvet. Outfit: oversized
off-shoulder cream-apricot chunky-knit sweater, one shoulder
slipped down. Skin: fair with warm undertones. Style: semi-realistic
anime, painterly, soft warm light (gold/cream/peach).

A continuous 12-second shot at dawn inside a serene anime-style
bedroom. The camera does NOT cut — it dollies smoothly forward from
the door perspective and finally pushes into a close-up of her
eyes.

[0-3s] WIDE ESTABLISHING SHOT: sheer white curtains half-drawn,
warm golden morning sunlight streams through, casting long
stripe-shaped light patches on a plush unmade bed with rumpled
white duvet. A subtle human silhouette visible under the covers.
A small white ceramic vase on a wooden nightstand. Slow forward
dolly from the door. Dust particles float slowly in the sunbeams.

[3-7s] MEDIUM SHOT: the catgirl Wen You reclining lazily in a pile
of fluffy white pillows, sweater slipping off one shoulder, tri-
color hair spread over shoulders and pillow, one hand lightly
touching her cheek, amber eyes half-lidded and dreamy. Slow
push-in toward her upper body. Soft side-back rim light catches
her hair edges.

[7-10s] CLOSE-UP on her head and ears: she tilts her head slightly,
tri-color cat ears gently twitch and rotate, ear-tip orange fur
glows translucent gold in direct sunlight, hair strands brush past
the ear edges. Fixed camera with subtle breathing micro-shake.

[10-12s] EXTREME CLOSE-UP on her amber eyes: half-lidded, misted
with sleep-water, a fingertip lightly touching her cheek, lips
slightly parted, long hair drapes over her collarbone. Soft side
light fills her face, gentle shadow in the eye sockets. Hold the
last frame as the ENDING keyframe (this frame will be reused as
the opening of the next segment).

Painterly anime, soft bokeh, cinematic, 4K, 24fps, warm tones,
low contrast, no cuts within the 12 seconds.
```

## 完整 Prompt(中文 · 备选/可灵中文模式)

```text
@温柚:二次元写实厚涂风,18 岁三花猫耳少女温柚。黑/白/橘三色渐变
长发,琥珀色眼眸半眯带睡意,蓬松三花猫耳(黑/白/橘三块),蓬松三
花尾巴此时藏在被下。米杏色露肩粗针织毛衣,一侧肩头滑落。皮肤暖
调,治愈系光影。

连续 12 秒单镜头(全程不切),从门口缓慢前推至眼部特写。

0-3 秒:卧室清晨全景。米白纱帘半掩,暖金色阳光投下长条光斑,床铺
凌乱,被窝有隆起人影,床头柜白色陶瓷花瓶,空气浮尘缓慢飘动,从
门口缓慢前推。

3-7 秒:温柚半靠白色枕头堆中景。毛衣滑下一侧肩头,三色长发散在肩
头与枕面,一手轻抵脸颊,琥珀色眼眸半眯朦胧。慢推至人物上半身,
侧方晨光形成柔光轮廓。

7-10 秒:猫耳特写。温柚轻偏头,三花猫耳随头轻晃,耳尖橘色绒毛在
直射阳光下根根分明泛金边。固定机位,带轻微呼吸感微颤。

10-12 秒:琥珀眼眸极特写。半眯蒙睡醒水汽,指尖轻抵脸颊,嘴唇微
张,长发垂落锁骨,柔光铺满面部,眼窝处留柔和阴影。**末帧保持 0.5
秒静止**(末帧将作为下一段开头的复用画面)。

整体调性:厚涂风,柔焦,4K,24fps,暖金色调,低对比度,治愈系。
```
