# 各分镜镜头视频生成提示词(英文模板)

> 配合 [[04 视频分镜脚本_90秒版]] 使用。
> 每个镜头提供:
> - 通用英文 Prompt(适配 Sora / Runway / 可灵 / 通义万相 / 智谱清影 / Vidu 等)
> - Negative Prompt(负面提示)
> - 工具适配建议(各平台对镜头/运镜/时长支持略有差异)

## 统一人设锚点(CHARACTER ANCHOR)

为保证角色一致性,**每个镜头 prompt 前都必须**包含以下设定段(可直接复用):

```
Character "Wen You": 18-year-old anime catgirl, half-human half-cat.
Hair: waist-length, tri-color (black, white, orange) loose waves, slightly messy.
Eyes: amber, half-lidded when sleepy.
Cat ears: tri-color fluffy, matching hair (black/white/orange patches), twitch gently.
Tail: long fluffy tri-color (black/white/orange), moves naturally.
Outfit: oversized off-shoulder cream-apricot chunky-knit sweater, bare feet or soft cotton shorts, later paired with cream knit long skirt.
Skin: fair with warm undertones, soft.
Style: semi-realistic anime, painterly, soft lighting, warm color palette (cream, gold, peach).
```

## 镜 01 — 全景空镜(5s)

**Prompt**:
```
Wide establishing shot of a serene anime-style bedroom at dawn.
Sheer white curtains half-drawn, warm golden morning sunlight streaming
through, casting long stripe-shaped light patches on a plush unmade bed
with rumpled white duvet. A subtle human silhouette is visible under
the covers. A small white ceramic vase sits on the wooden nightstand.
Dust particles float slowly in the sunbeams. Cream-white walls, wooden
floor, soft beige rug. Slow forward dolly from the door perspective.
Warm color palette, low contrast, gentle soft-focus, painterly anime
style, cinematic composition, 4K, 24fps.
```

**Negative Prompt**:
```
people, characters, harsh light, neon, modern furniture, dark scene,
low quality, blurry, text, watermark, deformed.
```

**运镜**:Slow dolly forward
**适配**:Sora / Runway Gen-3 / 可灵 1.5 / 通义万相 全部支持

## 工具适配速查

| 工具 | 单镜时长上限 | 是否支持运镜描述 | 备注 |
|------|:---:|:---:|------|
| **Sora** | 20s | ✅ 强 | 效果最好,优先 |
| **Runway Gen-3 Alpha Turbo** | 10s | ✅ 强 | 镜头语言成熟 |
| **可灵 1.5 (Kling)** | 10s | ✅ 中 | 中文场景理解好,镜头短需合并 |
| **通义万相 2.x** | 5-10s | ⚠️ 弱 | 建议简化运镜描述 |
| **智谱清影 CogVideoX** | 6s | ⚠️ 弱 | 适合短镜头组合 |
| **Vidu** | 8s | ✅ 中 | 国产替代,可对比 |
| **Luma Dream Machine** | 5s | ✅ 中 | 偏电影感 |

> **若单平台时长不足 5s 怎么办**(常见于可灵/通义万相 1.x):
> - 把相邻 2-3 个短镜头合并成 1 段 prompt,在 prompt 内用 "Scene A → Scene B" 描述转场
> - 或用剪映/CapCut/Premiere 把生成的多个短片段拼接,中间加 0.5-1s 叠化(溶解)转场

## 后期拼接建议(剪映 / CapCut / PR)

1. 顺序拼接所有镜头,**镜头之间加 0.5s 黑场或溶解(0.7s)**
2. 全程叠加一层 **"暖色 +10, 颗粒 5, 柔光 +15"** 的滤镜
3. 音轨:
   - 主音轨:一首治愈钢琴/吉他 BGM(80-90 BPM,例如 *Tsundere Cat*、*Nuvole Bianche* 类)
   - 副音轨:环境白噪(晨间鸟鸣 + 风铃 + 远处车流)全程低音量
4. 片头黑场 1.5s + 标题 "暖阳与三花猫少女"(可选)
5. 片尾黑场 2s + 淡出
